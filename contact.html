youngkive (영카이브)

is a digital portfolio platform featuring my work as a freelance graphic designer since 2022 and as a student at Queensland University of Technology (QUT).

The name combines “young,” taken from my Korean name Chaeyoung, and “archive.” The logo also incorporates the Chinese character 永 (Young), which symbolises a person swimming and the flow of water. It reflects my favorite quote, “Just keep swimming.”

Previously, I used to separate my university projects from my personal creative work. However, through this platform, I've brought them together using objects of a key and lock in the logo, visualising connection and continuity across my practice.
